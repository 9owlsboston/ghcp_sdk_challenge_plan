Suggested Questions for Q&A

Following each team’s presentation, the judges will get to ask 3 questions as part of the Q&A. Below are some sample questions based on our judging criteria.
 
1) Enterprise Applicability, Reusability & Business Value 
•	Who is the enterprise buyer/user and what measurable pain is solved?
•	How does this reuse across multiple enterprises with different stacks?
•	What is the ROI story (speed, quality, risk reduction) and proof?
•	What constraints (data, approvals, environments) could block adoption—and how are they addressed?
2) Integration with Azure & Microsoft Solutions 
•	Which Microsoft services are integrated—and why they are essential? 
•	What is the minimum integration path to still deliver value?
•	How does the solution degrade gracefully when integrations fail?
•	Can integrations be swapped/extended without rewriting core logic?
3) Operational Readiness: Deployability, Observability, CI/CD 
•	Deployment model fits enterprise needs?
•	Defined SLOs, scale assumptions, and performance targets?
•	Observability: logs, traces, metrics, audit trails—what does on‑call check first?
•	CI/CD maturity: tests, promotions, secrets, rollback strategy?
4) Security, Governance & Responsible AI 
•	Data handling: least privilege, minimization, retention, isolation?
•	Identity & auth model (users, apps, service principals, tool boundaries)?
•	Threats addressed (prompt injection, exfiltration, unsafe automation)?
•	Governance controls: policy, approvals, auditability, and admin controls?
•	Responsible AI: grounding, transparency, human‑in‑loop, limitations?
5) Copilot SDK & Agent Design Craftsmanship
•	Agent loop design (planning, tool choice, execution, reflection).
•	Grounding strategy (retrieval, structured context, and tool outputs).
•	Safe tool boundaries and policy‑aware actions?
•	Enterprise configurability via AGENTS.md / instructions (not hard‑coded)?
•	Quality evaluation over time (golden paths, tests, monitoring)?
