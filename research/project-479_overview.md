# Project 479 — "ME5 Get to Green" Overview

## What It Is

**Project 479 ("ME5 Get to Green")** is a targeted Security-focused STU (step-up/true-up) renewal campaign. It accelerates ME5 (Microsoft E5) renewals and pipeline conversion by giving sellers curated offers, technical proofing, and expert coaching, plus a repeatable engagement motion for eligible accounts.

The playbook positions it as a targeted STU renewal and upsell campaign with playbooks, offers, proof points, and expert help to accelerate ME5 conversations.

---

## Why It Exists

Security conversations require subject matter expertise that SAEs often don't have. The motion was adjusted to help SAEs listen for signal, validate interest, and then bring in Custom Solutioning Security Architects to move from interest to intent.

Sellers stay in control of the commercial/account motion while the program provides expert support when deeper security expertise is needed ("you don't need to be the security expert").

---

## Engagement Motion — Pod Model

Project 479 uses a regional "pod" approach to attach security solutioning support to the STU E5 motion. Each pod includes roles such as Strategic Consulting and Custom Solutioning (security). Sellers pull in the pod when the customer conversation requires security depth.
